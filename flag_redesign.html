Submit Your Flag Design for Good Counsel College!

Good Counsel College in New Ross, founded in 1828, has a rich tradition and history. However, despite being one of the oldest schools in the region, it still does not have an official flag. This is your chance to make a mark and help create the official flag for our school!

We are calling on all students, staff, and alumni to submit their ideas and designs. The design should reflect the school's heritage, values, and future aspirations.
